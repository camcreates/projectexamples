IMAGINE FSCJ

Affordable: FSCJ ranks among the top 25 most affordable public, four-year institutions in the nation. Plus, there are many financial aid opportunities available.

Convenient: We offer traditional, hybrid and online classes, some in accelerated formats.

Accessible: Classes are offered at seven convenient locations throughout Duval and Nassau counties and globally through distance learning.

Highly Ranked: FSCJ ranks 8th in the nation for the number of associate degrees awarded among two- and four-year institutions.

Diverse: We have students from more than 146 countries who range in age from 15 to 77 years old. FSCJ is also a military friendly school.
